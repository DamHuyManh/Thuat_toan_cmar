## Ghi chu

- `VERIFIED`: da mo duoc link dataset theo format moi (`/dataset/{id}/{slug}`), HTTP 200.
- `VERIFIED_INDEX`: da xac minh trang index UCI (`/datasets`) truy cap duoc, dung de tim dataset.
- Ghi chu kiem tra: cac link thu muc cu dang `.../ml/machine-learning-databases/...` hien tai tra 404 trong lan kiem tra nay.
